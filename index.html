About

I help mission-driven technology companies grow their customer base and build products that people love. I contribute to teams by using data-informed product, growth, and go-to market strategies, and (occasionally) front-end code.

Today, I'm studying at the University of Virginia Darden School of Business as a candidate pursuing a dual-MBA/M.Ed degree. Previously, I led product marketing at Panorama Education (YC'13), a company that's helping to improve student outcomes in K-12 schools through social-emotional learning assessment and analytics.

Before that, I created Speech4Good to help people (like me) with speech disorders access proven therapy tools through mobile apps. And before that, I studied political science and won a national championship with the lacrosse team at Tufts University.
